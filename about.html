The Beginning

What started as a quiet workshop in the heart of Colombo has blossomed into Sri Lanka's destination for unheated, museum-grade gemstones. Our founder's vision was clear: bypass middlemen, work directly with mines, and deliver authentic Ceylon sapphires, rubies, and cat's-eyes to collectors worldwide.

Every relationship we've built—with our mining partners, our master artisans, and our clients—is rooted in trust and transparency. We don't just sell gems; we preserve legacies.

How We Work

Three Pillars of Excellence

The Selection

We meticulously source only unheated, untreated gemstones directly from Sri Lanka's finest mines. Each stone is personally inspected by our gemologists to ensure museum-grade quality and exceptional provenance.

  • Unheated & Untreated
  • Direct mine sourcing
  • Rigorous quality assessment

The Craft

Every piece is hand-forged in our Colombo workshop by master artisans who've trained for decades. We work exclusively in 18k and 22k gold, employing traditional techniques refined across generations.

  • Hand-forged only
  • Premium gold grades
  • Bespoke customization

The Promise

Your piece comes with comprehensive international certification, detailed documentation, and our hallmark guarantee. We stand behind every creation with absolute confidence in its authenticity and craftsmanship.

  • Lab certification
  • Lifetime guarantee
  • Complete provenance
